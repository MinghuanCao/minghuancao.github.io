FAQ

What is GTO?

Game Theory Optimal (GTO) is a mathematically balanced poker strategy that cannot be exploited. Poker Solver Pro provides pre-computed GTO solutions so you can study and train optimal preflop play.

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings → Subscription → tap "Restore Purchases". Your subscription is linked to your Apple ID and will be restored automatically.

How does the free tier work?

Free users get access to:

  • All RFI (Raise First In) ranges for every position
  • vs RFI ranges for UTG, MP, and CO defenders
  • 25 trainer hands per day (resets at midnight)
  • Basic session statistics

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime through Settings → Subscription in the app, or via your Apple ID settings at Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions.

Is my data private?

Yes. All training data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. All GTO solutions are bundled with the app. No internet connection is required for browsing ranges or training.
